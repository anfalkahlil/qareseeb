Rare Ingredients

Discover the extraordinary components that make ZaFeeba fragrances unlike any other in the world.

Persian Saffron

Our Golden Saffron fragrance contains genuine saffron strands from Iran, the most expensive spice in the world. It takes over 150,000 flowers to produce just 1 kilogram of saffron, and we use only the highest grade available.

Sourced from the Khorasan region of Iran

Aged Cambodian Oud

Our oud is harvested from 100-year-old agarwood trees in Cambodia, then aged for a minimum of 30 years. This rare ingredient develops its complex aroma through a natural infection process that occurs in less than 2% of wild trees.

Sourced from the Cardamom Mountains, Cambodia

Bulgarian Damask Rose

The roses used in our Imperial Rose fragrance are harvested by hand at dawn in Bulgaria's Valley of Roses. The petals must be processed within hours of picking to preserve their delicate fragrance, requiring an entire field of roses to produce just a few milliliters of oil.

Sourced from Kazanlak, Bulgaria
